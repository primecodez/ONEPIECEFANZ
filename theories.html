Theories of One Piece

Some fans believe the One Piece is not a treasure but a concept—freedom itself.

Others think it’s a powerful ancient weapon or a message from Joy Boy.

🏴‍☠️ The One Piece Is Freedom

Many fans believe the One Piece isn’t a physical treasure, but the concept of ultimate freedom—Roger laughed because he understood its meaning.

⚔️ Joy Boy’s Promise

Some theorize that Joy Boy made a promise to liberate the world, and Luffy is the one destined to fulfill it—especially with the Nika fruit awakening.

🌍 Ancient Weapon Connection

Theories suggest the One Piece is tied to the ancient weapons—Pluton, Poseidon, and Uranus—and may be the key to reshaping the world order.
